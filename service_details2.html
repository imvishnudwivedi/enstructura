Structural Evaluation & Remedial Engineering

The division offers structural evaluation of various buildings/structural systems through detailed physical inspection, determination of engineering properties through non-destructive tests, carrying out detailed analysis & design of existing structural members, identification of inadequate members, if any, recommending appropriate remedial measures to ensure structural safety and durability of structural members.

Also division offers investigation of non structural members form the safety/durability considerations. The reason & extent of damage/deterioration will be assessed through detailed physical observation & appropriate non-destructive tests to recommend appropriate remedial measures from safety & serviceability considerations.

Structural evaluation / remedial engineering will be carried out for the following structures.

Our specialized diverse range of areas include
  • 1. Various R.C.C Structures
  • 2. Various Steel Structures
  • 3. Various Masonry Structures
  • 4. Various Hydraulic Structures
  • 5. Various Pre-stressed Concrete Structures
  • 6. Various Pre-Engineered Structures
