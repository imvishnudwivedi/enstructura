About



ENSTRUCTURA CONSULTANTS

Enstructura Consultants Private Limited (ECPL) is a consultancy service organization established by a group of professionals to offer comprehensive, innovative, state of art consultancy services with expertise developed over three decades having the capabilities to provide solutions across, Structural design and Proof checking, Structural evaluation and Remedial Engineering, Project management consultancy, Architectural design and Green building Engineering, Environmental Engineering and Engineering training. Our technical team is having handful experience to lead the project from inception through completion, controlling, planning, utility, efficiency, the project time, cost, monitoring quality and quantity.
